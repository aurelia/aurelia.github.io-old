  • Bundling an Aurelia Application

    Posted by AureliaEffect on June 23, 2015

    Aurelia is a next generation framework that promotes good design patterns and practices. The next version of HTTP, HTTP/2 [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HTTP/2], may very well obsolete the necessity of creating bundles in the future, but we are not there just yet. Nobody likes to see a production application making 256 requests when loading. That's why Aurelia takes bundling very seriously. Read on to find out how to get your app bundled up for deploy using our new CLI.
