Releases Notes Update - 9/7/2016

Posted by AureliaEffect on September 7, 2016

As always, we're working hard to squash bugs and make various improvements to Aurelia. We've had a number of minor and patch releases over the last week. Below you can read the details of what's been included in those releases.

Aurelia Validation

Aurelia Validation continues it's march towards beta. Today's release includes bug-fixes, usability improvements and a long requested feature: validate binding behavior support in custom attribute bindings. You can view the complete list of updates here .
