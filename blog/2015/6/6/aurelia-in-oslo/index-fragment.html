Aurelia in Oslo

Posted by AureliaEffect on June 12, 2015

Next week, Rob Eisenberg will be presenting at the Norwegian Developers Conference in Oslo, Norway. He'll be giving two presentations. The first, The New JavaScript: ES6 , is designed to bring you up to speed on the latest developments in JavaScript and help you catch a vision of where the language is heading in the future. The second talk, Aurelia: Next Generation Web Apps , will show you how a modern, ES6-based framework can make building complex JavaScript clients effortless. If you are in or around Oslo, we hope you will come and check it out.

Also, Core Team Member, K. Scott Allen , will be introducing Aurelia to the AngularJS Oslo Meetup on Tuesday evening. If you aren't able to attend NDC, then you also have this unique opportunity to hear from a great teacher like Scott about Aurelia .
