Aurelia in Stockholm

Posted by AureliaEffect on May 21, 2015

Next week, Rob Eisenberg will be be presenting at DevSum 2015 in Stockholm. He'll be giving two presentations on Aurelia. They function as a sort of "part one" and "part two" to help developers get up and going with the framework. On Wednesday, Tibi Covaci and Rob will be doing an all-day workshop on Aurelia as well. If you are in or around Stockholm, we hope you will come and check it out.
