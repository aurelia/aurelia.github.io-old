Aurelia Goes Full-time

Posted by Rob Eisenberg on October 17, 2019

Last week we hit a major milestone in Aurelia's history: one of our core team members, Fred Kleuver, was funded to work full-time on Aurelia.

Thanks to the generous financial contributions of many companies , we've raised enough money and have enough ongoing contributions to fund Fred Kleuver to work full-time on Aurelia for at least the next 6 months. We're hopeful that contributions will continue to grow during that time, and that we can continue to support Fred in the amazing work he's doing for well beyond this initial period.

We'd like to take this opportunity to give a special thanks to Hogia , our Gold Sponsor. Without Hogia and the many other financial contributors, this wouldn't be possible.

If you or your company is looking for an opportunity to contribute to open source, we would love for you to consider contributing to Aurelia . Help us continue to support Fred and to fund additional team members in full-time work. Together we can build the best front-end ecosystem and continue to help real companies deliver best-in-class solutions to their customers.

Thank you!
